About NEED

"The one place that brings a smile to your face"

What We Do

The Network for Environment and Energy Development (NEED) is a coalition formed by concerned activists and engineers at San Jose State University, that aims to develop a sustainable future focusing on spreading awareness, research and implementations of a wide array of topics including Conventional energy, Clean Energy, Sustainability, Environmental Conservation and much more. Our Goals

Our team of prudent environmental activists and concerned global citizens are constantly working to mutually reinforce and synthesize constructive solutions to issues victimized by Climate Change through scientific research, engineering, and sustainability.
